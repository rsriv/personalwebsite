Entrepreneurship

Technology is one of my primary interests. I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo building experience in the field through school, co-op and personal projects. You can read more about my projects in the projects section of the website. I enjoy software development and algorithm design and I strive to strengthen my software skill set at school and at home. I am interested in both software and hardware and I enjoy exploring the possibilities of computing in music.

I have been building my skills with the guitar and drums for years now and I'm always looking to take up new instruments. Many of my software projects involve music - including an algorithm to accurately create correct drum beats and the use of the JavaScript/HTML5 Audio API. I also love recording and producing music, and I have produced a song that has been played on local radio. Check out my SoundCloud here: soundcloud.com/crush_music

For me, entrepreneurship is a source of an excitement and energy paralleled by nothing. Except for maybe music. In 2013, I started BluesAndStuff Studios - a guitar school that provided structured, straightforward lessons to the Ottawa Area. I vividly remember the excitement as I designed the product (a customizable lesson plan) and built a client base. I look forward to having this feeling in the future as I start my next venture.
